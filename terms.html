Important Notice for Content Creators

At Shua Qhams, we value creativity and originality. We encourage all our users and content creators to respect the intellectual property rights of others.

Please refrain from copying or using videos created by other content creators without their explicit permission. Plagiarism not only undermines the hard work of original creators but also violates copyright laws.

To foster a vibrant and respectful community, we ask that you:

  • Always create original content or obtain permission before using someone else's work.
  • Give credit to the original creator when sharing their content.
  • Report any instances of copyright infringement you may encounter.
